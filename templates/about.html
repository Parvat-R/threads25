THREADS'25

A National Level Technical Symposium by the Department of Computer Science And Technology, Sona College of Technology

About the Event

Threads'25 is a premier technical symposium that brings together the brightest minds in technology. With a focus on innovation, creativity, and technical excellence, we provide a platform for students to showcase their skills and compete in various challenging events.

What to Expect

Experience a day filled with technical competitions, workshops, and non-technical events. From coding challenges to project presentations, Threads'25 offers diverse opportunities for participants to excel and win exciting prizes.

Theme

Under the banner of HACK · CREATE · DOMINATE, Threads'25 encourages participants to push their boundaries, innovate solutions, and emerge victorious in their chosen domains.

Venue & Date

Join us on February 27, 2025 at Sona College of Technology, Salem. Experience state-of-the-art facilities and participate in events designed to challenge and inspire.

Prize Pool

Compete for substantial prize pools across various events. Showcase your skills and win recognition along with exciting rewards. Each event offers unique opportunities to prove your expertise.

Why Participate

Gain valuable experience, network with fellow tech enthusiasts, learn from experts, and get a chance to win attractive prizes. Threads'25 is your platform to showcase innovation and technical prowess.
